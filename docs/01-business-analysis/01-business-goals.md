
# Phase 1: Business Analysis - VIXXY D'ORANCE
## 01. Business Goals

### 1.1 Primary Business Objectives
- Launch a luxury women's fashion e-commerce platform
- Achieve 10,000 registered users in the first 6 months
- Generate $500,000 in revenue in the first year
- Establish brand recognition in the Vietnamese luxury fashion market
- Provide exceptional customer experience with fast shipping and quality products

### 1.2 Secondary Objectives
- Build a loyal customer base with 40% repeat purchase rate
- Expand product categories within 12 months
- Integrate with major payment gateways (Momo, ZaloPay, Visa, Mastercard)
- Offer multiple shipping options (GHN, GHTK, Viettel Post, self-pickup)
- Provide real-time chat support for customers

### 1.3 Business Value Proposition
VIXXY D'ORANCE offers high-end, curated fashion products with:
- Premium quality and craftsmanship
- Exclusive designs and limited editions
- Personalized shopping recommendations
- Hassle-free returns and exchanges
- Secure and convenient checkout process
