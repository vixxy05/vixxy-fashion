
# Customer Personas - VIXXY D'ORANCE

## Persona 1: The Modern Professional
- **Name:** Nguyen Thi Minh Anh
- **Age:** 32
- **Occupation:** Marketing Manager at a multinational company
- **Location:** Ho Chi Minh City
- **Income:** 25-35 million VND/month
- **Shopping Habits:** 
  - Shops online 2-3 times per month
  - Values quality and brand reputation
  - Willing to pay premium for unique designs
  - Prefers fast and reliable shipping
- **Pain Points:**
  - Difficulty finding high-quality, unique clothing
  - Slow shipping delays
  - Lack of personalized recommendations
- **Goals:**
  - Find stylish, professional attire
  - Save time shopping online
  - Discover new designers and brands

## Persona 2: The Socialite
- **Name:** Pham Thi Ngoc Tram
- **Age:** 28
- **Occupation:** Freelance Event Planner
- **Location:** Hanoi
- **Income:** 15-25 million VND/month
- **Shopping Habits:**
  - Shops online 4-5 times per month
  - Follows fashion trends
  - Active on social media (Instagram, TikTok)

- **Pain Points:**

- **Goals:**
